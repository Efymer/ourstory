Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Introduction

OurStory ("we", "our", or "us") is a couples relationship app that helps you and your partner save places, create memories, plan dates, and track milestones together. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the OurStory mobile application.

By using OurStory, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Account Information
When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email from those services. We do not store your password — authentication is handled securely by our infrastructure provider.

Couple & Relationship Data
When you pair with a partner, we store your couple connection, invite codes, and optional relationship start date. Both partners can see all shared data within the couple.

Places & Memories
When you save a place or create a memory, we store the location name, address, coordinates (latitude/longitude), your notes, ratings, photos, and the date. This data is shared between both partners in the couple.

Photos
Photos you attach to memories or places are uploaded to our secure storage. They are accessible only to you and your paired partner.

Date Invitations & Milestones
When you plan dates or track milestones, we store the event details (place, date, time, notes, status) and milestone information (title, date, type).

Lists
Shared lists (grocery, wishlist, custom) and their items are stored and synced between partners.

Location Data
With your permission, we access your device location to show nearby places on the map and center the map on your position. We do not track your location in the background or store location history beyond the places you explicitly save.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the OurStory experience:

  • Display your saved places and memories on the map
  • Sync shared data between you and your partner in real time
  • Show nearby places and enable location-based features
  • Store and display your photos within the app
  • Send date invitations between partners
  • Track milestones and relationship dates
  • Manage your account and authentication

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

OurStory uses the following third-party services to operate:

Supabase — Database, authentication, and file storage. Your data is stored on Supabase's infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. Privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy

Google Maps — Map display and place search. When you search for places, your query is sent to Google's Places API. Google's privacy policy applies to map interactions: policies.google.com/privacy

Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In — If you choose to sign in with Apple or Google, those services handle authentication. We only receive your name and email; we never see your Apple or Google password.

We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs. No telemetry data is collected.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase's cloud infrastructure with the following protections:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Data is encrypted at rest on Supabase servers
  • Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure storage
  • Photos are stored in a private storage bucket with time-limited signed URLs
  • Row Level Security (RLS) ensures you can only access your own couple's data
  • No other user or couple can see your data

Data Sharing

Your data is shared only with your paired partner within OurStory. When you create a couple and pair with a partner, both partners have full access to all shared data (places, memories, lists, milestones, date invitations).

If you disconnect from a couple, all shared data is permanently deleted for both partners.

We do not share your personal data with any third parties except as required to operate the service (see Third-Party Services above) or as required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

Access — View all your data within the app at any time.

Correction — Edit your profile, places, memories, and other data directly in the app.

Deletion — Delete individual items (places, memories, milestones) or disconnect your couple to delete all shared data. You can also delete your account entirely from the app's profile settings.

Data Portability — Contact us to request an export of your data.

Withdraw Consent — Revoke location or photo permissions in your device settings at any time. The app will continue to work with reduced functionality.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), these rights are guaranteed under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). For users in California, additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) apply.

Children's Privacy

OurStory is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete that information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
