Delete Your OurStory Account

OurStory lets you delete your account and all associated data directly from the app.

In-app deletion (recommended)

  1. Open the OurStory app
  2. Go to Profile → Account → Delete Account
  3. Confirm twice. Your account and all its data are deleted immediately.

Email request (alternative)

If you cannot access the app, email support@ourstory.app with:

  • The email address associated with your account
  • Subject: "Account deletion request"

We will delete your account within 30 days of receiving the request.

What is deleted

  • Your user account (email, name, profile)
  • All memories you created (text, photos, location data)
  • All places, date invites, and milestones you created
  • Your push notification token
  • Your relationship/couple link (if your partner has the same couple, their access is revoked)

What is retained (briefly)

  • Payment records — Retained for up to 7 years as required by tax / financial regulations (Google Play Billing / RevenueCat invoices).
  • Server logs — Anonymized request logs may be retained up to 90 days for security and debugging.

Nothing personally identifiable is retained beyond these periods.

Questions? support@ourstory.app
